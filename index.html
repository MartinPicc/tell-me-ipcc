Tell me IPCC,

A semantic search engine to browse IPCC's report on climate change.

IPCC (Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change) is a United Nations institution in charge of studying climate change.

IPCC releases once every few years a comprehensive report summarizing latest scientific studies in a wide variety of fields related to climate change. Latest report has been published in 2014 and the next one is expected for 2022.
